# BUSINESS MODEL

Dealio earns money by generating qualified leads and referring them to providers.

Current vertical:

Automotive

Future verticals:

- Real Estate
- Insurance
- Loans
- Solar
- Services

Revenue Sources:

1. Dealer commissions
2. Featured listings
3. Advertising
4. Insurance referrals
5. Financing referrals
6. Subscription plans

Primary KPI:

Qualified leads converted into sales.

A lead is more valuable than website traffic.

The platform's purpose is:

Acquire → Qualify → Match → Refer → Track → Monetize

Everything must support this lifecycle.
